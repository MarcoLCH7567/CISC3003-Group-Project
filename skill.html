Peak Photography Tips | What do photographers do when they don't warm the bed in winter?

Edit: just a little sesameAuthor: sesame

It's getting harder and harder to get out of bed, but there's really no shortage of things to do, to eat, and to celebrate in winter.

Although people's ears are red from the cold in winter, when I think of candied haws, candied chestnuts and malatang, I immediately feel warm in my heart;

Although it is cold in winter, people do not want to move, but as long as I think of the warm sun and the lively scene on the street, I have the motivation to go out again];

Although it is cold in winter, people don't want to take their hands out of their pockets, but when they think of all kinds of beautiful scenery in winter, photographers can't help it anymore. …

Today, Xiaoai Photography has selected many "winter-flavored" photos from Xiaoai.com. Let's find "fire-like passion for life" in the cold winter photos together~
